Aster Mail is a privacy-first email client for astermail.org accounts.

Your messages are encrypted client-side before leaving your device. The server never sees your plaintext content - only you hold your keys.

Features:
- End-to-end encrypted mail between Aster users using a post-quantum ratchet protocol
- Zero-knowledge architecture: server stores only encrypted ciphertext
- Custom domain support with automatic DKIM signing
- Push notifications via UnifiedPush (no Google services required) with polling fallback
- Offline-capable with local encrypted storage
- Biometric lock
- Private mode notifications (hides sender and subject on lock screen)
- Quiet hours for notification scheduling
- Contact avatars with graceful fallback
- Attachment support with external app handoff
- AGPL-3.0 licensed

This F-Droid build contains no Firebase or Google Play Services dependencies. Push notifications are delivered via any UnifiedPush-compatible distributor (such as ntfy or Unified Push). Background polling provides a fallback when no distributor is installed.

Source code: https://github.com/Aster-Community/Aster-Mail
